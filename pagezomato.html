FOOD FESTIVAL BY ZOMATO

Date: September 3, 2024 | Time: 7:00 PM

Location: Phoenix Marketcity, Pimpri-Chinchwad, Pune

Get ready to embark on a gastronomic journey at the Zomato Food Festival, a culinary extravaganza that celebrates the diverse flavors of Pune.
This three-day event will bring together the city's top restaurants, food trucks, and street vendors, offering a unique opportunity to explore a wide range of cuisines and discover new culinary delights.
Beyond the food, the Zomato Food Festival will offer a vibrant atmosphere filled with live music, entertainment, and exciting activities for all ages. Enjoy live cooking demonstrations by renowned chefs, participate in food competitions, and explore the culinary market featuring local food products and artisanal crafts.
