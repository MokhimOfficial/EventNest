Creative Fusion: Pottery and Painting Workshop

Date: October 3, 2024 | Time: 10:30 AM

Location: Clay7Studio Pune

Unleash your creativity and immerse yourself in a day of artistic exploration at our Creative Fusion Workshop, where pottery and painting come together for an inspiring experience! This event is perfect for both beginners and seasoned artists looking to dive into the world of handmade crafts and visual art. Pottery Session: Kick off the day by shaping and molding your own clay creations. Under the guidance of our skilled pottery instructor, you’ll learn fundamental techniques such as hand-building and wheel-throwing. From crafting functional pieces like bowls and mugs to sculpting decorative items, you’ll have the chance to create your very own unique pottery.
Painting Session: After a break, dive into the vibrant world of painting. Our talented painting instructor will introduce you to various techniques and styles, whether you’re interested in watercolors, acrylics, or mixed media. Explore your artistic vision as you create a personalized painting on canvas, guided by expert tips and encouragement.
Join us for a day filled with artistic expression, new skills, and the joy of creating something beautiful from scratch. Whether you’re looking to try something new or hone your craft, this workshop is your chance to let your creativity shine!
