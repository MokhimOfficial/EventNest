The Soulful Collective - Pune's first Emotional Wellbeing meetup

This gathering is designed to create a safe space where we can explore, discuss, and enhance our emotional wellbeing together. Community Support: Connect with others who are on a similar journey towards emotional wellbeing, and share experiences in a non-judgmental environment. Practical Takeaways: Leave with actionable strategies to implement in your daily life for better emotional health
