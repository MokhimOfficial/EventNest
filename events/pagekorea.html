Korean Language Essentials: Duolingo Workshop

Date: September 30 to october 19, 2024 | Time: 01:30 PM

Location: Indo Korean Center Pune

Embark on an exciting journey into the Korean language with our Korean Language Essentials Workshop, powered by Duolingo! This workshop is designed for beginners and enthusiasts who are eager to start learning Korean in a fun and interactive way.
Introduction to Korean: Discover the basics of the Korean language, including the Hangul alphabet, essential vocabulary, and common phrases. Interactive Duolingo Sessions: Engage with Duolingo’s innovative learning tools and exercises. Learn how to navigate the app effectively to build your Korean skills at your own pace. Cultural Insights: Gain insights into Korean culture, traditions, and everyday life to enrich your learning experience and make your language practice more meaningful. Practice and Interaction: Participate in group activities and language practice sessions. Use what you've learned in real-life scenarios and practice speaking with fellow attendees.
All levels welcome! Whether you’re a complete beginner or have some basic knowledge of Korean, this workshop will provide you with practical skills and a strong foundation to continue your language learning journey.
Join us for this immersive and engaging workshop to kickstart your Korean language skills with Duolingo. Meet fellow language enthusiasts, enhance your learning experience, and have fun while discovering the beauty of Korean!
